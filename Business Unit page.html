Locations

Maryland

3700 Koppers Street, Suite 345
Baltimore, Maryland 21227
United States
(410) 715-7240

Exellar has been a driving force in the growth and development of Maryland, having successfully executed numerous projects across the State.

Our history in Maryland dates to 1927, when we completed our inaugural project - a warehouse for the American Stores Company located at Lafayette Avenue & Smallwood Street in Baltimore. In 2002, we cemented our presence and have become one of the largest construction management firms in the state.

Leveraging our local intelligence with Exellar’s global resources has enabled us to foster lasting relationships with our trusted clients and has earned us the reputation as the market leader in preconstruction and construction services.

From interior renovations to large scale new construction projects, our local team builds for the area’s largest institutions, public organizations, and private companies.

Beyond the concrete and rebar, we are committed to giving back to our communities through partnerships and outreach events that have a positive impact. Whether it’s supporting UBEs through the Exellar School for Construction Management, or fundraising for local charitable organizations, our team is committed to strengthening the communities within which we build.
