Why Work With Exellar

At Exellar, we are committed to fair and competitive compensating our employees fairly. We review salaries for U.S. staff on an annual basis at a minimum. We consider the impact on pay equity with every compensation decision, including when hiring, promoting, and managing our annual pay increase process. In addition, the Head Office Compensation team performs a comprehensive review independent from the Business Units to ensure salaries are both competitive with the external market and internally equitable.

Environmental, Social, and Governance Strategy

We support a healthy and more equitable future for our people, our business, and our planet through our ESG strategy.

Community & Citizenship

We work to leave a positive and lasting impact in the communities in which we build. Our commitments include economic development, service and outreach, youth engagement and workforce development. Learn more!

Diversity, Equity & Inclusion

Our vision is to be as richly diverse as the communities in which we work. We know that a diverse and inclusive workforce – one in which our differences are celebrated and our common values unite us – makes us stronger. Learn more!

Environmental Sustainability & Resiliency

As the largest green builder in the United States, we recognize that how we build is just as important as what we build. We conduct our business consciously and responsibly, protecting the health and safety our people and the environment. Learn more!

Innovation

Innovation starts with an audacious vision and a willingness to explore beyond what is into what could be. We are pioneering advancements that will reinvent how we work, improve people’s lives, and shape the future of the industry. Learn more!

Safety

We work hard every day to support and protect each other. We know that we are stronger together, and we are committed to creating the safest workplace possible for our people and partners. Safety is not just a priority, it's our culture. We take purposeful action to demonstrate the respect, empathy, and care we have for each other. Learn more!

Knowledge Sharing & Learning

Continuous learning is deeply rooted in our culture. Through site training, mentorship programmes, and hands-on experience, every team members are given opportunities to pursue more knowledge and grow their skills. 

On-site training is a core part of learning system that offers over 300 courses on topics from technical training to interpersonal skills to leadership development. The our internal knowledge sharing provides resources at your fingertips including playbooks, examples, templates and much more for the work that we do every day. Formal and informal learning opportunities continue to grow our staff. At Exellar, being a continuous learner is at the core of who we are as a company.

An excellent support system within Exellar to foster awareness, respect, and inclusion.

The Young Professionals Network fosters strong relationships and engagement in continuous learning to promote career advancement. We are creating the right environment by encouraging mentorship and transparency to build the future of our organization.

The Women's Network empowers and advocates for women to enhance their professional growth and visibility. We challenge unconscious bias and create the right environment for success.

The Mosaic Network empowers the career advancement of diverse employees. We are creating a community that fosters equity through awareness, advocacy, and intentional actions.

The Pride Network increases LGBTQ visibility and inclusion. We educate, advocate and empower people to maximize success.

Our team culture brings together people across all sites and offices. We create an environment where everyone is empowered to preserve our identity and use our skills and experiences to make Exellar a better place.

The Parents Network creates a community for Parents. We advocate for awareness and support of both mothers and fathers balancing the demands of work and raising a family by sharing experiences, resources and identifying areas of the organization where we can better support our working parents.

REASONABLE ACCOMMODATIONS
Exellar Construction LLP is an Equal Opportunity Employer. All qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, age, disability or veteran status, or any other applicable state or federal protected class. Exellar provides equal opportunity in employment for qualified Individuals with a Disability and Protected Veterans in compliance with Section 503 of the Rehabilitation Act and the Vietnam Era Veterans’ Readjustment Assistance Act. Federal law requires employers to provide reasonable accommodation to qualified individuals with disabilities. Please tell us if you require reasonable accommodation to apply for a job or to perform your job. Examples of reasonable accommodation include making a change to the application process or work procedures, providing documents in an alternate format, using a sign language interpreter, or using specialized equipment.  Applicants who need accommodation in the job application process should contact any Exellar office to request assistance.
