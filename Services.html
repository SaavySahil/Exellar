Built on Six Decades of Expertise

Our Services

Preconstruction

Our primary area of expertise. We undertake complete civil works for high-rise residential and commercial buildings — RCC structures, masonry, plastering, and lock-and-key finishing works. Advanced technologies like aluminium formwork enable faster cycles across G+40 storey developments.

Repairs & Rehabilitation

We have extensive experience in the repair and rehabilitation of existing structures — including structural strengthening, waterproofing, plastering, and restoration works aimed at enhancing durability and extending building lifecycle.

Our expertise allows us to address complex structural challenges and bring aging buildings back to full performance standards.

Structural Strengthening

We provide specialised services in structural strengthening, including reinforcement of existing RCC structures, retrofitting, and improvements to load-bearing capacity.

These services are particularly relevant for older buildings requiring compliance with updated safety standards or additional load requirements.

Waterproofing

Waterproofing is a critical and non-negotiable component of our service delivery. We offer comprehensive solutions for terraces, basements, facades, and all areas prone to water ingress — ensuring long-term structural protection.

Our waterproofing systems are selected to match the specific requirements of each project, delivering reliable performance through the building's lifetime.
